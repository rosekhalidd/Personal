Plan reference

Nutrition

~80 g protein/day (≈3 portions), 2 L water, carbs on training days, don't skip dinner. Easy foods: eggs, tuna, chicken, rice, pasta, salad, apple, cottage cheese, yogurt, PB toast, seafood out.

Training week

  • Sat — strength (dumbbells + bands)
  • Sun — strength
  • Tue — beginner calisthenics class
  • Wed — strength or mobility
  • Mon / Thu / Fri — rest + daily stretch

Strength moves (2–3 sets, 10–12 reps)

Goblet squat, dumbbell/band row, shoulder press, glute bridge, band pull-apart, Romanian deadlift. Warm up 5 min mobility every session.

Core — gentle / diastasis-safe only

Deep core breathing, heel slides, dead bug, bird-dog. Avoid crunches, sit-ups, full planks until the ab separation is checked by a professional.

Stretch (desk stiffness)

Hip flexors, chest/doorway, hamstrings, neck/traps.
